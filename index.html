About Us

Welcome to Catman Animal Shelter. We are dedicated to rescuing homeless cats and finding them loving homes.

Catman Animal Shelter was founded by Lawrence Lastra in 2017. He is a solo rescuer in the Philippines who aims to spread awareness about the importance of spaying and neutering stray cats and dogs. Lawrence spent his own money to care for the stray cats and dogs. A total of 150 cats and dogs have been castrated using his own funds.
